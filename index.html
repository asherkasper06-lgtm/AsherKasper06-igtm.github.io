About

Bio: Asher M. Kasper

I’m an undergraduate at Florida State University, majoring in International Affairs and currently in my first academic year (Class of 2029). I’m especially interested in global politics, information environments, and how languages and technology influence international relations.

I’m pursuing multiple language tracks — Russian, German, Spanish, and Arabic — and I plan to study abroad in Dresden in Summer 2026 to continue my German coursework. My current classes include topics such as Cold War science and technology, which have shaped many of my academic interests. I’ve also explored digital propaganda primarily in the context of the Israel–Hamas/Gaza conflict, which has helped spark my broader interest in online influence and media ecosystems, though this is an area I’m still developing.

Outside of coursework, I created the “DecodedFSU” media-literacy campaign, which brings together infographics, Instagram content, and a website as part of my ENC 2135 project. I also run K_AI Consulting, where I build tailored GPT-based tools for small businesses, including a specialized RealEstateGPT assistant for Florida real-estate contract drafting.

I’m originally from Florida, and I enjoy creative writing that explores themes of memory, justice, and surreal humor. I’m working toward combining my interests in policy, languages, and emerging technology as I continue through my degree.
